# Proposal Requirements Summary

This summary was extracted from the ECE 414 proposal template and should drive report preparation.

## Required major sections
1. Abstract
2. Table of contents
3. Introduction
4. Technical goals
5. Community impact and ethical issues
6. Standards, constraints, risks, and uncertainty
7. Design methodology, prototyping, validation, and hardware block diagram
8. Relevant technical background with one article and three patents
9. Marketability and lifecycle
10. Timeline and milestones
11. Conclusion
12. References

## Key constraints from the template
- include at least 3 to 4 relevant figures
- include at least 3 different standards
- include at least 3 major categories of constraints
- include a risk table with low, moderate, and high definitions
- include a hardware block diagram
- include one technical article review and three patent reviews

## Implication for this repo
- `docs/` should hold source material and planning for these sections
- `reports/proposal/` should hold the actual proposal drafts
- `reports/figures/` should collect reusable diagrams and plots
