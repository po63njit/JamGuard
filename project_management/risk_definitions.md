# Risk Definitions

This file standardizes how risks are labeled across milestones, reports, and weekly notes.

| Level | Meaning | Expected action |
|---|---|---|
| Low | Small issue with limited schedule or technical impact | Track it and resolve in normal workflow |
| Moderate | Material issue that may cause delay or rework | Add mitigation and review at the next checkpoint |
| High | Core threat to the build, validation path, or final demo | Escalate immediately and define fallback path |

## Current risk priorities
