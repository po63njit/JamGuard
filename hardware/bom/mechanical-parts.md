# Mechanical Parts

This list tracks the hardware required to build a repeatable five-antenna prototype.

| Item | Qty | Role | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Aluminum ground plane / rigid plate | 1 | Mechanical base and reference surface | In progress | Final dimensions depend on spacing study |
| Antenna mounts or adhesive fixtures | 5 | Hold antenna positions repeatably | Pending | Must resist movement during transport |
| Standoffs | Assorted | Set height and route cables cleanly | Pending | Record lengths once selected |
| Fasteners | Assorted | Assembly hardware | Pending | Standardize sizes to simplify rebuild |
| Strain-relief clips or ties | Assorted | Prevent cable motion and connector stress | Pending | Important for coherence repeatability |
| Enclosure / carry frame | 1 | Portable protection and transport | Deferred | Add only after prototype 1 geometry is frozen |

## Build rule
Any mechanical change that affects antenna position, cable routing, or ground plane geometry should be logged as a new prototype revision.
